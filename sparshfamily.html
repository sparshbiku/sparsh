About Us

  • Group of friends with a shared vision;do something good for the needy
  • We volunteer our time and donate for various causes
  • We do not have any management team
  • Contributing members decide where to spend dollars every year

Goals

  • 100% of donated funds go directly to the needy
  • No administrative fee, no maintenance fee etc
  • You are a leader if you become a member
  • You get to decide through voting where the money should go
  • Transparent accounting open to everyone how money is spent

Fundraising

  • Sparsh will collect funds from members who are interested in supporting various causes
  • Members decide which cause they want to support each year
  • Funds are allocated based need and priority
