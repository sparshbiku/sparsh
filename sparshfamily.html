About Us

We are a group of friends that have come together with a shared vision; ensure each cent of the money we donate each year reaches the needy. We do this by volunteering ourselves and encouraging friends/family to volunteer such that there is no administrative cost and even if there is one we pick up the tab ourselves. We do not have any management team. We are all Sparsh family members. Every person who joins the organization by donating as little as $5 monthly is a member and has voting rights to decide how to utilize the money that is collected.

Goals

We are a group of friends that have come together with a shared vision; ensure each cent of the money we donate each year reaches the needy. We do this by volunteering ourselves and encouraging frieds/family to volunteer such that there is no administrative cost and even if there is one we pick up the tab ourselves. We do not have any management team. We are all Sparsh family members. Every person who joins the organization by donating as little as $5 monthly is a member and has voting rights to decide how to utilize the money that is collected.

  • 100% of donated funds go directly to the needy
  • No administrative fee, no maintenance fee etc
  • You are a leader if you become a member
  • You get to decide through voting where the money should go
  • Transparent accounting open to everyone how money is spent

Fundraising

Sparsh Inc. will collect funds from individuals who are interested in supporting underprivileged kids and Old Age homes. All contributions will be exclusively utilized to fulfill the objectives as described above. Description of Fund Raising program: Sparsh Inc. will generate funds to support its objectives of supporting underprivileged kids and Old Age homes. For this purpose it will try to contact people in the community and educate them about the organization and request funds to be used for the above purpose. Several campaigns will be taken up to educate the community regarding the organization.
